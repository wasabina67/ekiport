# Statistics Card

## Overview
Display statistics for stations and airports.
Display in the lower left corner of the screen.
Click the label text to toggle marker visibility on the map.

## Requirements
1. [x] Statistics display
2. [x] Toggle visibility

### Statistics display
- Position: lower left corner of the map
- Show station count: e.g. `🚉 - 20`
- Show airport count: e.g. `✈️ - 21`

### Toggle visibility
- Click the station label to show/hide station markers
- Click the airport label to show/hide airport markers
- Reflect the on/off state visually (e.g. grayed out when hidden)
